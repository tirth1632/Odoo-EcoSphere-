Help Center & FAQ

Understand the calculation logic, data model mappings, and how to get help.

How is the overall ESG score calculated?
It is a weighted average of aggregated scores from Environmental (default 40%), Social (default 30%), and Governance (default 30%) modules. The calculation matches the settings parameters configured under the Settings panel.
How are emissions calculated automatically?
When "Auto Emission Calculation" is enabled, entering transaction logs based on emission sources (like Fuel or Grid Electricity) uses emission factors to automatically calculate total carbon values, instead of manual input.
Why can't I approve a CSR contribution?
If the "CSR Evidence Requirement" setting is toggled on, administrators cannot approve a contribution without a verified proof file path or file name.
How do Badge auto-awards work?
When "Badge Auto-Award" is toggled on, the system checks employee XP or challenges count upon action submission and automatically unlocks new badge items.
