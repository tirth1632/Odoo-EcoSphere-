Machine Learning Models

Artificial Intelligence at the Core

EcoSphere embeds production-grade ML models to forecast, categorize, and advise sustainability directors proactively.

AQI Prediction

LSTM neural networks predict Air Quality Index (AQI) based on regional atmospheric sensors and particulate levels.

Carbon Emission Forecasting

Prophet forecasting model projects monthly greenhouse gas emissions to warn managers of budget overruns.

Compliance Risk Prediction

Classification models flags departments at risk of regulatory audit failures based on open compliance tickets.

ESG Score Prediction

Simulates the impact of operational changes on the organization's corporate ESG score beforehand.

Department Performance Analysis

Unsupervised clustering identifies low-performing segments and benchmarks top ecological contributors.

Smart Sustainability Recommendations

Reinforcement learning suggests corrective offsets and gamified challenges customized per employee profile.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Learn more about the ESG calculation models, AI capabilities, and ERP integrations.

ESG stands for Environmental, Social, and Governance. It represents the three central factors in measuring the sustainability and ethical impact of an investment or business operation. EcoSphere quantifies these factors into live indicators.
ESG scores are computed from weighted inputs of carbon footprint records (Environmental), social engagement counts and employee CSR activities (Social), and completed compliance logs and audit filings (Governance). These weights can be customized dynamically by admins.
Our AI engine automates conversion of physical invoices into carbon equivalents, forecasts future carbon emissions, analyzes compliance risk margins, and dynamically gives sustainability suggestions tailored to specific corporate profiles.
All employee participation data, logs, and activity records are encrypted at rest and in transit. Standard RBAC (Role-Based Access Control) ensures that settings and details are restricted based on credentials and privileges.
Yes, EcoSphere is built to integrate natively with Odoo ERP modules (Purchase Orders, Manufacturing, Fleet, and HR modules) to automatically pull carbon invoices and activity ledgers without duplicate manual data logging.
